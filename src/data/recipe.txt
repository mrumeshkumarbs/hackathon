Vodka – Haku

Moscow Mule:

1 ½ oz Haku Vodka

½ oz Lime Juice

3-4 oz Ginger Beer

Combine ingredients in a tall cocktail glass or mule mug with ice and gently stir to combine. Garnish with a lime wedge.


Screwdriver:

1 ½ oz Haku Vodka

4-5 oz Orange Juice

Combine ingredients in a tall collins glass with ice. Garnish with an orange ½ wheel or wedge.


Sea Breeze:

1 ½ oz Haku Vodka

1 ½ oz Grapefruit Juice

3 oz Cranberry Juice

Combine ingredients in a rocks glass full of ice. Garnish with a lime wheel or wedge.


Bay Breeze:

1 ½ oz Haku Vodka

1 ½ oz Pineapple Juice

3 oz Cranberry Juice

Combine ingredients in a rocks glass full of ice. Garnish with a lime wheel or wedge.


White Russian:

1 oz Haku Vodka

1 oz Coffee Liqueur

1 oz Cream

Combine Haku and coffee liqueur in a rocks glass full of ice. Float the cream over top of the cocktail. No garnish.


Classic Vodka Martini:

2 ½ oz Haku Vodka

½ oz Dolin Dry Vermouth

Optional: 1 dash Orange Bitters

Combine ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir to chill and strain into a martini or coupe glass. Garnish with a lemon twist, oils expressed over the drink.

Sazerac:

2 oz Knob Creek Rye

½ oz Simple Syrup

6 dashes Peychaud’s Bitters

Absinthe, to rinse the glass

Pour absinthe into a cocktail glass and add 2-3 pieces of ice. Set aside.

Combine Knob Creek Rye, simple syrup, and bitters in a mixing glass with ice. Stir to chill. Swirl ice and absinthe in cocktail glass to coat the inside of the glass and dump contents of glass into a sink. Fill glass with chilled Sazerac cocktail. Garnish with a lemon peel, expressing the oils into the drink, and discard peel.



Japanese Whisky – Toki

Japanese Highball

Fill a tall cocktail glass with ice. Pour Toki over ice and gently stir to chill the whisky. Add more ice as needed, and fill the glass with soda water, stirring again to fully combine. Garnish with a grapefruit peel, oils expressed over the drink.
